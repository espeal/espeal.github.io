Live Lightly

An Application Idea I Designed to Showcase my Skills

As globalization has continued to evolve our planet as a whole, an integral mechanism of this has detrimental effects on our environment: Transportation. When weighing the options when it comes to travel, cost, time, and efficiency are usually the main variables. With flights becoming cheaper and more frequent it's hard to justify spending a similar amount on a 6 hours train versus a 1 hour flight. As an example, Eurostar commissioned independent research to assess the CO2 per passenger produced by a London-Paris Eurostar journey versus that emitted by a passenger on a London-Paris flight. The conclusion was remarkable: Taking the train to Paris instead of flying cuts CO2 emissions per passenger by a staggering 90%. My application aims to showcase the size of the individuals carbon footprint (utilizing inputted factors of: flights taken). These inputted flights taken could be taken from the user inputting throughout the year, or at one specific instance. It then calculates the number of metric tons needed to offset the amount of CO2 used and offers websites where you can offset this amount via a range of projects that include carbon avoidance, clean and renewable energy generation. It's a small step that can make a huge difference in slowing speed at which climate change is occurring.

Designed using Adobe Photoshop

About Me

My professional journey has been shaped by my passion for user experience design, website design, and people management—blending creativity and leadership in meaningful ways. I love the challenge of leading projects and team, mentoring others, and creating an environment where people feel inspired to do their best work.

At the same time, I’m deeply invested in crafting great digital experiences. UX design motivates me to think creatively and prioritize the journey of a user, ensuring every interaction feels smooth and intentional. Website design can be utilized to communicate ideas and connect with people visually and functionally.

Through these combined interests, I aim to create harmonious digital spaces and lead dynamic teams towards innovative solutions that resonate with both users and stakeholders alike.

Attributes

Organized

Effective organization and time management are the cornerstones of my professional approach. Recognizing the importance of a well-structured workflow, I meticulously organize tasks, priorities, and deadlines to ensure optimal productivity. By employing strategic time management techniques, I strive to maximize efficiency without compromising the quality of my work.

Self Motivated

I take on every challenge head-on and do what I need to accomplish my goals, even when the challenge is difficult. Every time I am given a new task, whether it's leading a team project or meeting a quick deadline, I always enjoy stepping up and demonstrating my skills.

Enthusiastic

I've chosen to work within the UX space because of my passion for both front end development and design. My enthusiasm is what drives me in every project I work on and every scenario I am given.

Creative

I’m a creative person. I love to integrate new skills and ideas into every project I tackle. Problems don’t unsettle me – they inspire me.

Adaptable

My flexibility to adapt to the demands of a job has allowed me to meet or even surpass my supervisor’s expectations. I have learned that work conditions change day-to-day and task-to-task. Projects I have worked on in the past can vary between requiring individual attention and requiring a team approach.

Hard Working

One of the first things I learned early from my parents was that hard work is the key to success. I recognize that working hard makes the difference and I put in the hours and effort to make sure that I am demonstrating my best work.
